Giant Panda

The giant panda's distinct black and white markings have two functions: camouflage and communication. Its white markings help it hide in snowy habitats, and the black helps it hide in shade.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Length: 160centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Temperate Forests

Distinctive black and white coat, with black fur around their eyes and on their ears, muzzle, legs and shoulders. Adult pandas are about 150cm from nose to rump and can grow up to 90cm tall at the shoulder, and they can weigh up to 150kg.
